Werner's New Million Mile Safe Drivers and Roadstar® Honorees

July 06, 2020
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Werner System, Inc. is pleased to announce its Class of 2019 Million Mile Safe Drivers and Roadstar® honorees. 

This year Werner is celebrating 143 business capacity owners (BCOs) for their safety records and professionalism behind the wheel. BCO is Werner’s term for an independent owner-operator who provides transportation capacity to a Werner motor carrier under an exclusive lease agreement.

The Class of 2019 safe drivers is comprised of 14 new Werner Roadstar® honorees, 117 new One Million Mile Safe Drivers, 10 new Two Million Mile Safe Drivers and two new Three Million Mile Safe Drivers. Each of the owner-operators has driven at least one million consecutive miles without a preventable accident.

Of Werner’s 1,086 active Million Mile Safe Drivers, 14 individuals were recognized this month for earning the Roadstar distinction. The Roadstar designation is one of Werner’s highest honors for truck owner-operators awarded to the “best of the best” on their high levels of safety, productivity and excellence in customer service. Each of the 14 Roadstars previously earned Million Mile Safe Driver status.

“Werner is proud to showcase the 2019 Class of Werner Million Mile Safe Drivers and Roadstar honorees as the industry’s best. Their skill and dedication to their profession makes the roads safer for all of us,” said Werner President and CEO Jim Gattoni. “Werner Roadstar recipients raise the bar for the entire trucking industry and their dedication to service excellence has earned them the right to be called the most elite independent business capacity owners at Werner.”

The new class raises the total number of active Werner Roadstar recipients to 178. Werner began presenting the Roadstar award in 1990.
