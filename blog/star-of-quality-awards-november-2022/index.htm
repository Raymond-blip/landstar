Star of Quality Awards - November 2022

January 19, 2023
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

When independent Werner business capacity owners (BCOs) and agents go above and beyond, their efforts are recognized with Werner’s Star of Quality award.

Each month, deserving BCOs and agents earn Werner’s Star of Quality Award for the outstanding efforts they make every day. Our thanks and praise to these members of the Werner network, who represent the best in the freight transportation industry.

If you would like to nominate a Werner agent or BCO for a Star of Quality Award, please email StarQ@Werner.com.

Congratulations to the Werner Star of Quality Award winners in November!
