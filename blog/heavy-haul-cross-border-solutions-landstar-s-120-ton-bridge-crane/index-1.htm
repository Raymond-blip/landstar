Meeting Critical Deadlines for Heavy Haul Cross-Border Shipments

  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email
This content was originally published on InboundLogistics.com. 

When moving over-dimensional freight across borders, precision and reliability are crucial. For a Fortune 200 energy manufacturer whose operations in Mexico support U.S infrastructure projects, timely cross-border transport is vital. Delays can disrupt multimillion-dollar jobs. To meet these high-stakes demands, the manufacturer depends on Landstar's exclusive, 120-ton overhead bridge crane, well-positioned at the border’s most active gateway in Laredo, Texas. This crane ensures seamless transloading for heavy haul freight, reinforced by Landstar’s strong focus on safety, security, and service.  

The Case for Transloading

Without transloading, heavy haul shippers face logistical challenges tied to finding suitable equipment,

 available on the right dates, operated by a reliable provider with proper service and insurance. Those with irregular routes and inflexible schedules face even more difficulties.  

Landstar's crane-powered transload operation in Laredo allows for the use of Mexican trailers for transportation within Mexico and U.S. capacity for domestic delivery. This flexibility ensures job sites remain on schedule, turning a logistical hurdle into a strategic advantage. 

Anchored in Safety & Security

The handling of heavy, high-value, or over-dimensional freight necessitates rigorous safety and security protocols. Landstar’s overhead bridge crane, designed specifically to accommodate these loads – from turbine blades to industrial generators, reflects its commitment to a safety-always culture. Anchored in place for reinforced structural integrity, the crane is operated by trained and certified personnel, ensuring professional handling of goods. 

The crane is located in Landstar’s CTPAT-validated Laredo facility which operates under the U.S. Customs and Border Protection’s security standards.  Featuring advanced protective measures, including 24/7 security, guarded truck entry, restricted building access, and intrusion detection fencing, offering shippers peace of mind. 

End-to-End Service Advantage

Landstar's comprehensive approach eliminates the need for multiple third-party providers, simplifying the process significantly. Independent agents manage the shipment from start to finish, providing a single point of contact for specialized transportation needs. 

Efficiency is further enhanced by Landstar’s network. While shipments are en route, the Laredo team coordinates the crane appointment, enabling transloading to occur within two hours of arrival. This cohesive process ensures challenges are addressed seamlessly and swiftly. 

Key Takeaway

From Mexico to the U.S., Landstar offers heavy haul shippers dependable, efficient solutions, backed by a fully managed process that delivers confidence, reliability, and peace of mind. 

Don’t leave critical deadlines or high-value freight to chance. Contact Landstar to streamline your cross-border shipments with unmatched safety, security and service.

Landstar’s Bridge Crane Advantage

Landstar’s 120-ton bridge crane is the only one of its kind in Laredo and is built to handle the largest cross-border loads, with a clearance area of 35 feet high and 37 feet wide. It offers heavy haul shippers an option that prioritizes safety, efficiency and savings.
