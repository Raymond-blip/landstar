Werner Agent Rookie of the Year Announced

April 21, 2020
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Werner awarded the 2019 Rookie of the Year Award to independent Werner Agent Louis Rios Jr. this month. Werner defines a rookie as an independent agent who is new to the Werner network, but not necessarily new to the transportation industry.

“It is Louis’ experience, professionalism and determination that earned him the Rookie of the Year recognition,” said Werner President and CEO Jim Gattoni. “During his first year in operation, Louis has demonstrated outstanding revenue performance and customer service excellence.”

Since joining the Werner network in August 2018, the Louis Rios Agency, based in Helendale, California, has generated more than $2.6 million in Werner revenue. Rios was first introduced to logistics during the 13 years he spent in the United States Army. Later, as a civilian, he used his military experiences while working for a third-party logistics company, before opening his own independent Werner agency.

Werner Rookie of the Year Award recipients must be part of Werner for less than two years, demonstrate an outstanding revenue performance and encompass excellence in customer service. Nominees for the 2019 award opened an independent Werner agency between July 1, 2018, and June 30, 2019.
