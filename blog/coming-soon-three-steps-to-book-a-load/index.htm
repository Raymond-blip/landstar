Coming Soon: Three Steps to Book a Load

July 26, 2017
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Werner’s brand new Available Loads app is coming soon to Android and iPhone. The mobile app is designed to give business capacity owners -- Werner’s term for independent truck owner-operators -- a better, easier way to find and book loads.

The free mobile app was designed for the needs of BCOs and was first announced during Werner’s All-Star Celebration in Nashville, Tennessee in July. That same month, the app was put to the test and tweaked to fully support Werner owner-operators’ needs.

Soon, booking a shipment will easily be done in three steps:

  1. Login using your name and password from WernerOnline.com
  2. Easily set your load parameters and search loads
  3. Simply tap to call the agent and book your load


“We went through multiple designs and gathered feedback on the app’s functionality from Werner BCOs throughout the process. Our goal was to refine the app to do what owner-operators expect it to do with ease,” said Werner Executive Vice President of Capacity Development Rocco Davanzo.

The new Available Loads app is intuitive and has the same features and loads that can be found on WernerOnline.com. With a simple download, the app will give BCOs an easy-to-navigate load board right on their phones.

“BCOs won’t see fewer loads than they would see on the load board or miss anything when they shift from the web version to the mobile app,” explained Davanzo.

The Available Loads app is available in your smartphone's playstore.
