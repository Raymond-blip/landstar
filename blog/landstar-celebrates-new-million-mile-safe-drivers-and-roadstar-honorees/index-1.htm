Landstar Celebrates New Million Mile Safe Drivers and Roadstar Honorees

July 16, 2024
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Landstar honored the best and safest Landstar independent owner-operators at the 2024 Landstar BCO All-Star Celebration on July 7.

During the 2024 All-Star Celebration, Landstar inducted the newest class of Million Mile Safe Drivers and Roadstar® honorees. The Class of 2023 is comprised of 109 new One Million Mile Safe Drivers, 20 new Two Million Mile Safe Drivers, one new Three Million Mile Safe Driver and one new Four Million Mile Safe Driver. Each of the owner-operators has driven at least one million consecutive miles with Landstar without a preventable accident.

Of Landstar’s 1,062 active Million Mile Safe Drivers, including those inducted in this class, 13 individuals were recognized for earning the Roadstar distinction. The Roadstar designation is one of Landstar’s highest honors for truck owner-operators awarded to the “best of the best” for their high levels of safety, productivity and excellence in customer service. Landstar began presenting the Roadstar award in 1990. Currently, there are 172 active Roadstar honorees.

“Driving a million miles or more without a preventable accident is no easy feat. Landstar takes great pride in celebrating the skill, safety records and professionalism behind the wheel demonstrated daily by these men and women who are dedicated to their profession as independent owner-operators,” said Landstar President and CEO Frank Lonegro.

“Landstar Roadstar honorees take safety to the next level, setting the benchmark for the entire trucking industry in their unparalleled dedication to service excellence. They have earned the right to be called the most elite independent owner-operators at Landstar,” Lonegro said.
