Manage Fuel Expenses at the Click of a Button

October 03, 2019
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Only two months after its release, more than 8,500 owner-operators are using the WernerOne™ mobile app to save money and make better business decisions. With daily fuel searches exceeding 146,000, the mobile technology is making a positive cost-savings impact for Werner independent business capacity owners (BCOs)*.

“The fuel feature on WernerOne helps me make better decisions because I know exactly what I’ll have to pay,” says Werner BCO Greg Huggins. “It’s quicker than checking multiple sources and doing the math to figure out my fuel savings and IFTA credit.”

According to ATBS, an accounting, bookkeeping and tax company for professional truck drivers, fuel costs are one of the largest expenses for most owner-operators.

“The app instantly provides accurate information on fuel prices across state lines, which takes a lot of the guess work out of my trip and fuel planning,” added Huggins.

Some independent owner-operators leased to Werner have reported saving up to $90 each time they fill up, since using the mobile app.

WernerOne™ is a portal for all of Werner’s exclusive mobile applications and is designed as a one-stop business tool for owner-operators. In addition to the app’s fuel search feature and IFTA tax information, WernerOne provides access to:

  • Thousands of available loads on Werner’s load board
  • Preferences for Load Alerts® notifications
  • Reviewing and creating back-to-back loads for multi-leg full week runs through the Werner Maximizer® tool

WernerOne is available for download for iPhones in the App Store or for Android phones on Google Play. Once downloaded, BCOs can access the mobile app using their WernerOnline username and password. If you have questions about the WernerOne app, please email WernerOne@Werner.com.

*Business capacity owner (BCO) is Werner's term for independent owner-operators exclusively leased to Werner.
