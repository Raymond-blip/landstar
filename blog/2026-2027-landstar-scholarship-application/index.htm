2026-2027 Werner Scholarship Application

November 03, 2025
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Is your child a college student or senior in high school who is successful in and out of the classroom and whose qualities exemplify Werner’s commitment to excellence?

Applications to the Werner Scholarship Fund for the 2026-2027 academic school year are now available.

The deadline to apply is March 2, 2026.

Click to download the 2026-2027 Freshman Scholarship Fund Application

Click to download the 2026-2027 Sophomore, Junior & Senior Werner Scholarship Fund Application

The Werner Scholarship Fund was established to recognize and reward outstanding undergraduate academic achievement and civic involvement of children of Werner employees, independent business capacity owners (BCOs) leased to Werner and independent sales agents. Since 1995, Werner has awarded 429 scholarships totaling more than $1.15 million.

Scholarships are awarded based on a student’s:

  • Academic excellence
  • Community service
  • Extracurricular activities

Eligible candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • Scholarships are only available to children of full-time Werner employees, independent BCOs and independent agents, as well as employees of BCOs and agents currently under contract with a Werner company. Children of deceased Werner employees, BCOs or agents who died while employed by or under contract with a Werner company are also eligible for scholarships. Children of Werner officers are ineligible to apply for the scholarship fund.
  • Eligible candidates must be enrolled in high school full-time and be planning to be enrolled full-time (12 or more credit hours/semester) at an accredited two- or four-year college or university, or must already be enrolled in a two- or four-year college or university and have successfully completed a minimum of 12 credit hours before the application deadline (March 2, 2026.)
  • All candidates must also be planning to be enrolled full-time (12 or more credit hours per semester) at their college or university during the academic year for which the scholarship is granted.
  • All candidates must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher.

Download the proper application, complete and mail to:

Werner Scholarship Fund Scholarship Applications
Attention: Don Stambaugh
13410 Sutton Park Drive, South
Jacksonville, FL 32224

All materials must be received no later than the date specified on the scholarship application. The deadline to apply for the Werner scholarship for the 2026-2027 academic year is March 2, 2026.
The total number of scholarships awarded in any year cannot exceed 25% of the total number of applications received. The number of scholarships to be awarded will be determined following the scholarship application deadline.

Once the applications are received, an independent selection committee will evaluate all the applications and choose scholarship recipients. Award amounts are typically between $2,500 and $5,000 per scholarship for the upcoming school year.

Awards are announced each spring during the Werner Agent Convention. All applicants should receive a mailed letter by the end of April 2026 advising if they are selected as a 2026-2027 Werner Scholarship recipient. If you submit an application and do not receive a notification by the end of April, please send an email to DStambaugh@Werner.com.
