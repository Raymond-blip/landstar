2025 Sponsor Spotlight

November 21, 2025
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Thank you to our 2025 event sponsors, whose contributions helped make this year's annual Werner Agent Convention and the Werner BCO All-Star Celebration a memorable experience for our business capacity owners (BCOs) and independent agents.

Werner's diamond-level sponsors include Comdata, Gallagher Insurance, Insurance Office of America (IOA), and Pilot Flying J. Their contributions helped us honor our accomplished independent agents who met revenue milestones and owner-operators who have gone above and beyond in their dedication to service. Thanks to the support of these sponsors, we’re able to highlight the commitment to safety, security and service excellence of our independent business owners each year.

Sponsor Comdata, Inc. is a leading provider of innovative payment and operating technology that drives actionable insights from spending data, builds enhanced controls and positively impacts its clients' bottom lines. Comdata's technical capabilities in credit card issuing and transaction processing have helped independent businesses with electronic payment programs for decades.

For over 30 years, Gallagher Transportation Services has offered clients insurance and risk management products. Their assistance gives valuable coverage for freight solutions. Gallagher is one of the world's largest insurance brokerage, risk management, and consulting firms that has met clients' needs.

Event sponsor IOA offers unique insurance coverage, including high-value trip, transit, and cargo insurance policies, which are instrumental in helping clients plan for current and future liabilities.  IOA has knowledge of the transportation and logistics industry, enabling its team to help clients plan for current and future liabilities.

Pilot Flying J offers Werner's independent owner-operators exclusive fuel rebates at locations across the country. As North America's largest network of travel centers with 750 locations, Pilot Flying J supplies more than 14 billion gallons of fuel annually to 1.5 million guests with trusted rewards services. 

Once again, thank you to all the sponsors who helped support this year's events and continue to support Werner entrepreneurs every day through discounted services.
