LandstarOne® Mobile App Now for Third-Party Carriers

September 09, 2022
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Landstar-approved carriers now have access to thousands of load opportunities through the LandstarOne® mobile app.

Download the LandstarOne® app to:

  • Access thousands of load opportunities with Landstar Load Search
  • Streamline the booking process with important load details including revenue, rate per mile, load dates and times, weight, miles, load age, and load comments available right in the app
  • Find the best fuel prices, including SavingsPlus® fuel discount details
  • See your fuel price net of IFTA credit by location

Download LandstarOne® on the App Store or on Google Play.
