Werner Listed on Inbound Logistics G75 List

June 21, 2024
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via LinkedIn
  • Share via email

Werner System, Inc. was recognized on the Inbound Logistics 75 Green Supply Chain Partners list for 2024.

According to Inbound Logistics, “From 3PLs to air cargo carriers, and from ports to truckers, Inbound Logistics recognizes 75 supply chain visionaries who have come full circle by demonstrating their commitment to sustainability every day in every way.”

Some of Werner’s efforts that led to this selection include:

Aerodynamic Devices

As of 2023, 99.96% of Werner’s van trailers are equipped with aerodynamic devices. Each of the aerodynamic features used on its van trailers is listed on the SmartWay Verified List of Aerodynamic Devices as providing EPA-verified fuel savings of 5% or more when used in conjunction with low-rolling resistance tires. 

Low-Rolling Resistance Tires

100% of Werner's new trailer purchases are equipped with low-rolling resistance (LRR) tires. Werner is also committed to replacing any existing tires with LRR tires, depending on availability at the replacement location.

Remote Tracking Devices

In recent years, Werner has upgraded thousands of single-use battery-powered trailer tracking units to rechargeable battery-powered and/or solar-powered units. By the end of 2023, 13,034, or over 96%, of its van trailers were equipped with these advanced tracking devices.

Werner Savings Programs

Werner encourages the use of the WernerOne® mobile app, which includes Load Alerts® and Werner Maximizer® technologies, to minimize empty miles and reduce idling time. Furthermore, Werner independent owner-operators are motivated to adopt technologies and purchase products that enhance fuel efficiency. For instance, the Werner Contractors’ Advantage Purchasing Program (LCAPP) offers discounts on items such as low-rolling resistance tires and auxiliary power units.

As Werner President and CEO Frank Lonegro explains in the company’s 2023 Corporate Sustainability Report, “Werner’s employees and independent entrepreneurs are deeply committed to having a positive impact – through safety, environmental stewardship, and community involvement. Werner will continue to keep these principles and efforts at the forefront of all we do.”

Read more about Werner’s sustainability efforts in our 2023 Corporate Sustainability Report.
