Safety & Security

As a safety-first company, our initiatives include strict Landstar operator qualification requirements, orientation and training, plus customer and public outreach programs.

Tools & Technology

Landstar’s scale, systems and support, combined with our proprietary technology, help us to provide unparalleled value to our customers. Our technology and mobile apps make it easier to coordinate freight shipments. We are a leader in digital freight management, not mere digital load matching.

With technology like the LandstarOne® mobile app, capacity providers can easily find and set Load Alerts® notifications for potential loads from our freight board that meet their criteria.

We’ve developed technology for agents that provides real-time data about their businesses and trends in their customers’ businesses. The results are tools that provide agents with in-depth analytics, solid data on pricing, and access to available capacity so they are empowered to provide superior service to customers.
