Tools & Technology

Werner’s scale, systems and support, combined with our proprietary technology, help us to provide unparalleled value to our customers.

The Werner system is built to support agents, owner-operators, other third party capacity providers and customers with tools and technology to help them successfully run their businesses. Werner doesn’t just adapt to technological advancements – we’re leading the charge.

Our technologies, ranging from analytics and pricing tools for agents to freight search and available loads mobile apps for truck capacity providers, empower our network of entrepreneurs to deliver customized transportation solutions to customers.

Agent Analytics: In-depth business analytics help agents manage their businesses better, with access to up-to-date data and graphs about revenue, productivity, customers and predictive analysis.

Werner Maximizer®: Owner-operators find the best combination of back-to-back available loads for themselves, creating options for multi-leg, full-week runs right from their smartphones.

Werner Clarity™: Werner’s proprietary freight-tracking and exception-management tool enables Werner independent freight agencies to monitor shipment progress and provide solutions to potential disruptions quickly. Agencies can provide their customers a link to real-time tracking information for freight visibility anytime they need it.

Pricing Tools: Our proprietary technology integrates historical and real time pricing data with additional outside data, so agents can react faster to emerging market trends.

WernerOne®: The WernerOne® app gives owner-operators access to their LCAPP® fuel savings, the Werner Maximizer® and Available Loads apps, and WernerOnline.com.

Designing Bold New Technology

Werner’s ability to pioneer the industry starts with our method of developing technology. Before designing a mobile app or new technology, we conduct focus groups and beta testing with agents and owner-operators to assess their needs in order to better meet their goals. The continuous feedback is incorporated into the functionality of our technology to ensure it truly meets the needs of our network.
