How does it work?

Business capacity owners (BCOs), our term for independent owner-operators leased to Werner, have access to our load board 24/7. Werner is 100% non-forced dispatch so you choose what and when to haul. We support you every step of the way with business and safety classes as well as an exclusive cost-savings program – the Werner Contractors’ Advantage Purchasing Program (LCAPP). Through the LCAPP® program, you’ll receive discounts on tires, fuel, equipment, business services and more.
