Agents

Landstar agents are independent business owners with the tools and support of a multibillion-dollar company and the experience to provide custom shipping solutions and dedicated customer service. 

Become An Agent

Landstar is entirely agent based. Our independent agents provide customers with personalized service at the local level with access to Landstar’s vast resources that provide the global support, stability, and strength of an industry leader.

Flexibility

Our independent agents have the freedom to operate their business in a manner that is best for you – giving them the ability to provide custom solutions for even the most unique challenges. Whether you need freight moved across the country or across the border, independent agents with Landstar can help facilitate the move.

Dedicated Customer Service

As independent business owners, a Landstar agent’s livelihood depends on earning and keeping their customers. They have a vested interest in your satisfaction and have the backing of the entire Landstar network to make that happen.

Experience

Landstar agents are independent, but they aren’t alone. When agents partner with Landstar, they have the backing and support of specialized departments from compliance to finance, so they can help you every step of the way.

How does the agent model work?

Landstar’s independent freight agents work with customers to find the best solutions for their transportation challenges. Landstar provides agents with back office support, experience, access to capacity and a well-known brand. Agents coordinate the transportation plan, secure capacity through the connections of the Landstar network of 78,000 capacity providers, and communicate with customers every step of the way.
